# Dynamic SOF (Special Operations Forces) Campaign

### Mission Statement

This project is meant to be a single-player/coop dynamic mission generator mod/scenario for Arma 3 with milsim style elements.  With Special Operations Forces mission in mind,  I want to take a elements from other SP/Coop missions I've played and add a sense of realism and variety to them.  I like the full-map feeling of Antistasi, like an entire world is moving around you while you attempt to complete your mission. I like the performance of Dynamic Recon Operations (DRO) and Dynamic Combat Operations (DCO) in terms of only generating what is needed to keep the gameplay very smooth.  I have also always had a love of the immersion elements of Call of Duty campaigns.  My goal is to try and capture all of the good things out of those three examples without having the poor performance that other scenarios get.

As far as the campaign elements, get an area setup on the map with units is one thing, adding immersion elements such as why the units are there, or why the player has to go to an area is another.  Eventually I'm hoping to make these campaign elements AI generated for variety.
