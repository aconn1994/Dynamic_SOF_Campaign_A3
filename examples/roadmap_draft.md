# Dynamic SOF Campaign (DSC) Roadmap
Full name: Dynamic Special Operations Forces Campaign

## Purpose

I've always loved two things in Arma 3, singleplayer campaigns (East Wind, Apex, Hunter Six) and coop scenarios (Dynamic Recon Operations, Antistasi, Forgotten Few). I love the immersion of singleplayer campaigns and the replayability of the coop scenarios, and I'm hoping I can take the elements from those, as well as some of the attributes of a skilled zeus and put them into a scenario mod to give the feeling of being in the Special Operations Forces.

I've put together a rough outline of the phases I envisioned to try and make this a fun mod with alot of playability:

## Phase 1: Locations
### Military
    - Camps (small)
    - Outposts (medium)
    - Bases (large)

### Civilian
    - Villages (small)
    - Towns (medium)
    - Cities (large)

### Special (Maybe map specific, these will be open ended and most likely need to be determined by creator in the editor)
    - Airbases would be a large location
    - Factories, Quarries, Radio Stations on Altis, or the Sugar Cane factory on Tanoa would be medium
    - Small would be office buildings in Pyrgos or Kavala on Altis, or villas on Malden

## Phase 2: Mission Types
### Types
    Direct Action:
        - Kill/Capture
        - Hostage Rescue
        - Asset Seizure
        - Destroy
    AFO:
        - Recon
        - Surveillance
        - Hack
        - Sabotage
    Influence/Foreign Internal Defense/Village Stability Operations
        - Search/Cordon Area
        - Patrol with Conventional/Partner Force Troops

## Phase 3: Factions
### Groups:
    Currently at a basic level.  We determine available groups in the factions based on the internal Arma config using a tagging system.  Eventually we can do this at a mod level so we can incorporate Aegis/RHS/3CB/Legion Studios.
### Units:
    Also at a basic level, used in spawning static emplacements.  Will be extended for classes of vehicles and other singular entities that will make playthroughs fun and dynamic

## Misc/Experimental Phases
### World Building
    - Determine who is occupying the map, who are the players friends/enemies and potential random encounters
### Intel System
    - When doing a mission, intel will determine players next actions and add a sense of purpose and immersion to missions
### Supports
    - Gives the players a higher ratio of support elements as special operations forces would have in real life as force multiplier
    - Air support, ISR, Infil/Exfil platforms etc
### Briefing/Story Telling (Super Experimental)
    - This one is really experimental and maybe impossible, but I was thinking about trying to build an extension that can hit an AI API and build campaign/mission briefings as an immersion element.
    - For example, if we just did an AFO mission of seizing a radio antenna from enemy forces and "hacking" it (which in the mission sense, would be attacking the radio station and then defending it while hacking takes place), the follow on mission after that would be a mission based on the intel we got from the hack.  And I figure GPT or another AI would be able to come up with really fun world building briefings and scenarios to add a campaign element.
